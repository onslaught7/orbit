# Orbit

## What is Orbit and what problem does it solve?
In the modern day and age, even in cities with a dense population count, high social media usage, top job portal sites and interesting dating apps, still the actual socialization lacks. Especilly for the group of youngster new into jobs and still navigating the complexities of health, finances, relationships, jobs and what not while trying to find their place in this world. 

You could still be having all the social media applications and still be lonely. You could still be surrouded by tens of hundereds of people, people you see everyday but never talk and never have the chance to socialize.

Now the crowd might not be what you want to be a part of, but there is a chance, people around you already exist who think like you, who want to have a life experience like you, who have the same hobbies, past times or life goals as you have. The only difference being you never take the chance to actually figure out and know all of that.

It could be lack of time and energy, social anxiety etc. But what if you could already know what people's lives have in common around you with yourself and something that you could bond over.

For the fellow techies out there think it as a social connection optimization engine.
You want to find people around you who you would love to share your time and energy with then you have to initiate communication, talk to them over and over, invest efforts only to find out this is not what I was looking for and you repeat the whole thing again.

We give the hashmap to you. The keys with the values. Based on the values you chose which key is the right one for you. Through this platform of ours you can find
- the next business partner.
- the next co-founder.
- the right employee.
- the right recruiter.
- the dream partner.
- the gym bro.
- the next door neighbor who loves the same obscure band as you.

Guess what, these are people around you so once you connect just directly meet up and start you beautiful moments over the alignment you already have.

## Who is Orbit for?

Primary user: 21-27 year olds navigating early career life in Indian metros. 
New to a city, new to a job, or simply new to adulting. They have ambitions, 
interests, and a limited social circle that doesn't fully reflect who they are 
or where they're headed. They're not antisocial — they're time-poor and 
connection-starved.

They're not looking for followers. They're looking for people.

## What does success look like?

Version 1 success: 10 friends using it, 3 real connections made through it. 
One conversation that wouldn't have happened otherwise. That's the bar.
